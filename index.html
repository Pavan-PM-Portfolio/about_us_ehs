Who we are

At EduHubSpot, we don’t just teach — we transform. Every course is designed to blend real-world project management expertise with the latest in learning innovation, so you can gain skills that pay off immediately in your career.

From PMP® Certification to advanced project leadership programs, our mission is simple: help you pass the exam once, and lead for a lifetime.

The vision & mission behind EduHubSpot

Two friends started with one belief: great training should be simple, personal, and built around the learner. Here’s what still drives every course we build.

Our Vision

To guide a period of global leadership in Career-Advancement Certification Programmes, EduHubSpot aimed at driving learners from across the world to achieve their career dreams through our cutting-edge learning programmes, dedicated mentors, and continuous support.

Our Mission

Our mission is to equip the working professionals through innovative expertise, knowledge, and experience which are vital for the global Project Management Workforce to attain their desired outcomes and remarkable success.

Meet the team behind your success

From product to mentorship, sales to support — this is the team that works behind the scenes to ensure your learning journey is smooth, guided, and outcome-driven.

2012 · The SparkWhere it all beganIt started in 2012 with one goal: pass the PMP. Varun — the son of a retired mathematics professor — was in his late twenties when he cleared it. The celebration was simple and unforgettable, and with it came a quiet realisation: this exam wasn’t the finish line. It was the start of everything.

2013 · The Struggle’Varun, you’re too young’Certification in hand, Varun knocked on the door of every training company he could find — and was turned away, again and again: too young. He refused to give up. A year later a call finally came, and he delivered his first-ever training in Cleveland, Ohio. The students loved it. He never got paid, but he kept teaching anyway — he wasn’t chasing a paycheck yet, he was building a name.

2016 · EduHubSpot is BornTwo friends, one visionThe money was coming, but Varun wanted to build something that would outlast him — and he couldn’t do it alone. He sat down with his best friend and roommate Chirag, a software developer, and EduHubSpot was born. Chirag got his hands dirty in the code; Varun recorded lesson after lesson in a green-screen studio they rigged up in their own basement. From that basement, the first thousands of students found them.

2017 · The First ClassThe first classBefore the studio and the thousands online, there was one small room — a projector, a stack of PMP guides, and a handful of people who took a chance on a brand-new name. When they held up their certificates, EduHubSpot stopped being an idea. It became a school.

2020 · Going GlobalThe world went onlineThe trainings had gone global, and so had the team — Varun crossing borders and airports to teach in person, then moving the whole company online when the pandemic arrived. Life kept moving too: a new baby on his lap while a room full of learners waved back from the laptop screen. Growth and home, at exactly the same time.

Today · Still BuildingFrom a basement to a global classroomThe basement green-screen became a real studio and a real team. Today EduHubSpot is a PMI Authorized Training Partner that has guided 20,000+ professionals to the careers they’d always dreamed of — still taught by mentors who’ve walked the path, still built by two friends chasing the same thing they always were.
